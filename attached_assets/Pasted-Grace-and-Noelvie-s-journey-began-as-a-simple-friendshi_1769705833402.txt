Grace and Noelvie’s journey began as a simple friendship in the year of 2015— but it was truly the answer to a whispered prayer from both Grace and Noelvie.

What first caught Grace’s eye was a photo of Noelvie, standing behind the counter at Wendy’s, wearing her Wendy’s cap and filled with hard work and perseverance. Even in that simple uniform, she looked effortlessly beautiful — her bright smile, her focused eyes, and the warmth she radiated made him want to know the heart behind that pretty face and hardworking spirit. And all these years later, that same beauty still captivates him every single day.

Their first date was at a Chinese Buffet restaurant . On the way there, Noelvie, who was very sick and had a runny nose, didn’t have a tissue. Without hesitation, Grace gently used his own hands to wipe her nose for her — a small, unthinkable, tender act that showed Noelvie his true heart: caring, selfless, and real. That night, they sat together sharing good food and endless laughter, and felt an instant cconnection that made them both realize they’d found something rare.

Their second date was just as meaningful. They sat together in a parked car under the night sky, watching a sermon and sharing dreams about putting God first — deciding that faith would always be the foundation of their love.

After two beautiful years of growing together, Grace asked Noelvie to be his fiancée at a traditional engagement filled with family and friends. From that moment, they continued to build their bond through honest love, friendship, and faith — taking their time to truly know one another before saying I do.

Now, after several wonderful years of knowing each other, growing together, laughing, praying, and loving deeply, Grace and Noelvie are ready to share their joy with family and friends through their traditional marriage celebration and white wedding following after— honoring what began in quiet prayers and gentle promises.

And if you’re wondering who said I love you first — of course, it was Grace!
